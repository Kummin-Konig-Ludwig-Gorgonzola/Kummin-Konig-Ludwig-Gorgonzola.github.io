founding ☭

The republic of goddard is a micronation founded in 2022 by Tombo, Caremelised Onion Cheese, Bread Cheese and Beans Cheese. at its founding, they took upon the cheesanese culture and swore loyalty to the people and to the creation of a classless society. from here, the departments and the initial draft of the constitution were formed, as well as the bestowment of the duty of käse kaiser and politburo. since then, we have grown to a mighty nation with many citizens with great roles.

chabis

A chabi is a tenth of a day, and it is our main system of time in the goddard. Goddardians will likely count in decimillichabis, and they are less than the imperialist time system's seconds.
